# Quick Reference: Scoring Summary

## **Daily Points:**

- 2+ hours training: 10 pts
- Easy challenge: 5 pts
- Medium challenge: 15 pts
- Hard challenge: 30 pts
- Expert challenge: 50 pts
- Writeup: 10 pts
- Review writeups: 5 pts
- New technique: 10 pts
- Update cheat sheet: 5 pts
- Timed practice: 5 pts

## **Weekly Bonus Points:**

- Live CTF participation: 50 pts
- Each CTF solve: 20-50 pts (difficulty based)
- Team session: 25 pts
- Help teammate: 15 pts

## **XP Conversion:**

- 1 point = 10 XP

## **Target Scores:**

- Daily: 50-100 points
- Weekly: 400-700 points
- Monthly: 2000-3000 points (Gold tier)
