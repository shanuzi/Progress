## XP Earning Rules

- Training hour: 10 XP
- Easy challenge: 50 XP
- Medium challenge: 150 XP
- Hard challenge: 300 XP
- Expert challenge: 500 XP
- Writeup created: 100 XP
- Live CTF participation: 200 XP
- Live CTF solve: 300 XP per challenge
- Helping teammate: 150 XP
- Learning new technique: 100 XP

## Level Thresholds

- **Level 1-5 (Beginner):** 1000 XP per level
- **Level 6-10 (Intermediate):** 2000 XP per level
- **Level 11-15 (Advanced):** 3000 XP per level
- **Level 16-20 (Expert):** 5000 XP per level
- **Level 21+ (Elite):** 10000 XP per level
